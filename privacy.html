Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Venix Partners Limited ("we", "us", or "our"), a company incorporated under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, collects, uses, stores, and shares your personal information when you use the BingeBay platform ("Platform" or "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023, the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR), and other applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity Information: Email address
  • Account Information: Username, password, profile picture, content preferences, and channel subscriptions
  • Usage and Interaction Data: Viewing activity, watch history, search queries, content likes, and feedback
  • Technical Data: IP address, device identifiers, device type, operating system, browser type, referral URLs, and geolocation data
  • Communications: User communications with our support team, survey responses, and marketing preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To create, verify, and manage user accounts
  • To enable access to content and services available on BingeBay
  • To process viewer payments and Creator payouts securely and efficiently
  • To personalize content recommendations and enhance user experience
  • To analyze user behavior and improve platform performance, functionality, and security
  • To provide technical support, resolve disputes, and respond to user inquiries
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To send service announcements, updates, and promotional communications (only where consent is given)

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We rely on the following legal bases to collect and process your data:

  • Consent: For marketing and non-essential cookies or features
  • Contractual Necessity: To deliver services you request or subscribe to
  • Legal Obligation: To meet obligations under applicable laws (e.g., tax, anti-fraud, AML laws)
  • Legitimate Interests: For platform optimization, analytics, and ensuring user safety

4. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We may disclose your personal data in the following scenarios:

  • To vetted service providers and partners who assist us in operating our platform, including hosting, analytics, and payment services
  • To regulatory authorities, law enforcement, or government bodies when required by law or legal process
  • To third-party merchants or service providers, with your explicit consent or where you engage with them through the platform
  • To third parties involved in a corporate transaction (e.g., merger, acquisition), subject to confidentiality

Important: We do not sell or rent personal information to marketers or unrelated third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated in this Policy, including:

  • While your account remains active
  • As required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., financial recordkeeping, fraud prevention)
  • Until consent is withdrawn or you request erasure, subject to legal exceptions

6. Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Secure servers and firewalls
  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Regular vulnerability assessments and platform audits
  • Limited access controls to personal data based on role necessity

Despite our best efforts, no system is completely secure. Users should take reasonable steps to protect their credentials.

7. International Data Transfers

  • If personal data is transferred outside Nigeria, such transfers are conducted with adequate safeguards as required under the NDPA and NDPR
  • This may include standard contractual clauses, data protection agreements, and secure hosting in compliant jurisdictions

8. Your Data Protection Rights

Under Nigerian data protection law, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access – Request access to your personal data and receive a copy
  • Right to Rectification – Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent – At any time, without affecting prior lawful processing
  • Right to Erasure – Request deletion of your data, subject to legal limitations
  • Right to Object – Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
  • Right to Data Portability – Request transfer of your data to another service provider

Requests must be submitted through the contact information below. Verification of identity may be required.

9. Children's Privacy

BingeBay is not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect or process data from minors. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the data immediately.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, understand usage patterns, and deliver personalized content
  • You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. For more information, please refer to our Cookie Policy

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. When changes are material, we will notify you through the platform or via email
  • Continued use of BingeBay after changes are posted indicates your acceptance of those updates
